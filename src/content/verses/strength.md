Feeling weak or worn out? These Bible verses remind us that true strength comes from God, who renews, sustains, and upholds us in every season.

## 💪 7 Bible Verses for Strength

### 1. Philippians 4:13
<blockquote data-ref="Philippians 4:13">
I can do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me.
</blockquote>

---

### 2. Isaiah 40:31
<blockquote data-ref="Isaiah 40:31">
But they that wait upon the Lord shall renew their strength; they shall mount up with wings as eagles; they shall run, and not be weary; and they shall walk, and not faint.
</blockquote>

---

### 3. Psalm 46:1
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 46:1">
God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.
</blockquote>

---

### 4. 2 Corinthians 12:9
<blockquote data-ref="2 Corinthians 12:9">
And he said unto me, My grace is sufficient for thee: for my strength is made perfect in weakness. Most gladly therefore will I rather glory in my infirmities, that the power of Christ may rest upon me.
</blockquote>

---

### 5. Nehemiah 8:10
<blockquote data-ref="Nehemiah 8:10">
Then he said unto them, Go your way, eat the fat, and drink the sweet, and send portions unto them for whom nothing is prepared: for this day is holy unto our Lord: neither be ye sorry; for the joy of the Lord is your strength.
</blockquote>

---

### 6. Psalm 28:7
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 28:7">
The Lord is my strength and my shield; my heart trusted in him, and I am helped: therefore my heart greatly rejoiceth; and with my song will I praise him.
</blockquote>

---

### 7. Exodus 15:2
<blockquote data-ref="Exodus 15:2">
The Lord is my strength and song, and he is become my salvation: he is my God, and I will prepare him an habitation; my father's God, and I will exalt him.
</blockquote>

---

## 🙏 Prayer

God, be my strength when I feel weak. Renew my spirit and help me rely on You each day. Amen.
