Feeling afraid or uncertain? These Bible verses remind us that God is with us, holding our hand and giving us strength to stand firm in faith.

## 🔥 7 Bible Verses for Fear

### 1. Isaiah 41:13
<blockquote data-ref="Isaiah 41:13">
For I the Lord thy God will hold thy right hand, saying unto thee, Fear not; I will help thee.
</blockquote>

---

### 2. Joshua 1:9
<blockquote data-ref="Joshua 1:9">
Have not I commanded thee? Be strong and of a good courage; be not afraid, neither be thou dismayed: for the Lord thy God is with thee whithersoever thou goest.
</blockquote>

---

### 3. Psalm 34:4
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 34:4">
I sought the Lord, and he heard me, and delivered me from all my fears.
</blockquote>

---

### 4. Deuteronomy 31:6
<blockquote data-ref="Deuteronomy 31:6">
Be strong and of a good courage, fear not, nor be afraid of them: for the Lord thy God, he it is that doth go with thee; he will not fail thee, nor forsake thee.
</blockquote>

---

### 5. Psalm 23:4
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 23:4">
Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I will fear no evil: for thou art with me; thy rod and thy staff they comfort me.
</blockquote>

---

### 6. Psalm 56:3
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 56:3">
What time I am afraid, I will trust in thee.
</blockquote>

---

### 7. Romans 8:15
<blockquote data-ref="Romans 8:15">
For ye have not received the spirit of bondage again to fear; but ye have received the Spirit of adoption, whereby we cry, Abba, Father.
</blockquote>

---

## 🙏 Prayer

Lord, when fear rises within me, remind me that You are near. Help me trust in Your presence and walk in courage, knowing You will never leave me. Amen.
