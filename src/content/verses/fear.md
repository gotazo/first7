Fear can feel overwhelming, but God reminds us that we are never alone. These verses will help you stand strong in faith.

## 🔥 7 Bible Verses for Fear

### 1. Isaiah 41:13
> For I the Lord thy God will hold thy right hand, saying unto thee, Fear not; I will help thee.

---

### 2. Joshua 1:9
> Be strong and of a good courage; be not afraid... for the Lord thy God is with thee whithersoever thou goest.

---

### 3. Psalm 34:4
> I sought the Lord, and he heard me, and delivered me from all my fears.

---

### 4. Deuteronomy 31:6
> Be strong and of a good courage, fear not... for the Lord thy God... will not fail thee.

---

### 5. Psalm 23:4
> I will fear no evil: for thou art with me.

---

### 6. Psalm 56:3
> What time I am afraid, I will trust in thee.

---

### 7. Romans 8:15
> Ye have not received the spirit of bondage again to fear.

---

## 🙏 Prayer

Lord, help me trust You when I feel afraid. Replace my fear with Your peace. Amen.
