When you feel low or overwhelmed, God’s Word brings hope and light into darkness.

## 🌿 7 Bible Verses for Depression

### 1. Psalm 34:18
> The Lord is nigh unto them that are of a broken heart.

---

### 2. Psalm 42:11
> Why art thou cast down, O my soul? hope thou in God.

---

### 3. Isaiah 41:10
> Fear thou not; for I am with thee.

---

### 4. Matthew 11:28
> Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden.

---

### 5. Lamentations 3:22–23
> His compassions fail not.

---

### 6. John 16:33
> In the world ye shall have tribulation: but be of good cheer.

---

### 7. Psalm 147:3
> He healeth the broken in heart.

---

## 🙏 Prayer

Lord, lift my spirit and remind me that You are near. Amen.
