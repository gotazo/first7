Struggling with heaviness, sadness, or feeling overwhelmed? These Bible verses remind us that God is close to the brokenhearted and brings light even in the darkest moments.

## 🌿 7 Bible Verses for Depression

### 1. Psalm 34:18
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 34:18">
The Lord is nigh unto them that are of a broken heart; and saveth such as be of a contrite spirit.
</blockquote>

---

### 2. Psalm 42:11
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 42:11">
Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted in me? hope thou in God: for I shall yet praise him, who is the health of my countenance, and my God.
</blockquote>

---

### 3. Isaiah 41:10
<blockquote data-ref="Isaiah 41:10">
Fear thou not; for I am with thee: be not dismayed; for I am thy God: I will strengthen thee; yea, I will help thee; yea, I will uphold thee with the right hand of my righteousness.
</blockquote>

---

### 4. Matthew 11:28
<blockquote data-ref="Matthew 11:28">
Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest.
</blockquote>

---

### 5. Lamentations 3:22–23
<blockquote data-ref="Lamentations 3:22–23">
It is of the Lord’s mercies that we are not consumed, because his compassions fail not. They are new every morning: great is thy faithfulness.
</blockquote>

---

### 6. John 16:33
<blockquote data-ref="John 16:33">
These things I have spoken unto you, that in me ye might have peace. In the world ye shall have tribulation: but be of good cheer; I have overcome the world.
</blockquote>

---

### 7. Psalm 147:3
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 147:3">
He healeth the broken in heart, and bindeth up their wounds.
</blockquote>

---

## 🙏 Prayer

Lord, lift my spirit and remind me that You are near, even in my lowest moments. Help me find hope in Your Word and strength in Your presence. Amen.
