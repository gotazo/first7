Love is at the heart of the Bible. God’s love for us is constant, and He calls us to love others in the same way. These verses reveal what true love looks like.

## ❤️ 7 Bible Verses About Love

### 1. 1 Corinthians 13:4–5
> Charity suffereth long, and is kind; charity envieth not; charity vaunteth not itself, is not puffed up,  
> Doth not behave itself unseemly, seeketh not her own, is not easily provoked, thinketh no evil.

---

### 2. John 3:16
> For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life.

---

### 3. 1 John 4:7
> Beloved, let us love one another: for love is of God.

---

### 4. 1 John 4:19
> We love him, because he first loved us.

---

### 5. Romans 5:8
> But God commendeth his love toward us, in that, while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us.

---

### 6. Colossians 3:14
> And above all these things put on charity, which is the bond of perfectness.

---

### 7. Proverbs 10:12
> Hatred stirreth up strifes: but love covereth all sins.

---

## 🙏 A Simple Prayer

Lord, teach me to love as You love—patiently, kindly, and selflessly. Help me show love in my words and actions each day. Amen.
