Feeling weary or uncertain about the future? These Bible verses remind us that true hope is found in God, who renews our strength and fills us with peace.

## 🌅 7 Bible Verses for Hope

### 1. Jeremiah 29:11
<blockquote data-ref="Jeremiah 29:11">
For I know the thoughts that I think toward you, saith the Lord, thoughts of peace, and not of evil, to give you an expected end.
</blockquote>

---

### 2. Romans 15:13
<blockquote data-ref="Romans 15:13">
Now the God of hope fill you with all joy and peace in believing, that ye may abound in hope, through the power of the Holy Ghost.
</blockquote>

---

### 3. Psalm 39:7
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 39:7">
And now, Lord, what wait I for? my hope is in thee.
</blockquote>

---

### 4. Lamentations 3:24
<blockquote data-ref="Lamentations 3:24">
The Lord is my portion, saith my soul; therefore will I hope in him.
</blockquote>

---

### 5. Hebrews 11:1
<blockquote data-ref="Hebrews 11:1">
Now faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen.
</blockquote>

---

### 6. Isaiah 40:31
<blockquote data-ref="Isaiah 40:31">
But they that wait upon the Lord shall renew their strength; they shall mount up with wings as eagles; they shall run, and not be weary; and they shall walk, and not faint.
</blockquote>

---

### 7. Psalm 71:5
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 71:5">
For thou art my hope, O Lord God: thou art my trust from my youth.
</blockquote>

---

## 🙏 Prayer

Lord, fill my heart with hope and help me trust in Your plans. Renew my strength and remind me that my future is secure in You. Amen.
