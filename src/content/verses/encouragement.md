Feeling discouraged or weary? These Bible verses remind us that God strengthens, uplifts, and walks with us through every season.

## 💛 7 Bible Verses for Encouragement

### 1. Joshua 1:9
<blockquote data-ref="Joshua 1:9">
Have not I commanded thee? Be strong and of a good courage; be not afraid, neither be thou dismayed: for the Lord thy God is with thee whithersoever thou goest.
</blockquote>

---

### 2. Psalm 31:24
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 31:24">
Be of good courage, and he shall strengthen your heart, all ye that hope in the Lord.
</blockquote>

---

### 3. Isaiah 40:29
<blockquote data-ref="Isaiah 40:29">
He giveth power to the faint; and to them that have no might he increaseth strength.
</blockquote>

---

### 4. Romans 8:28
<blockquote data-ref="Romans 8:28">
And we know that all things work together for good to them that love God, to them who are the called according to his purpose.
</blockquote>

---

### 5. Hebrews 10:24
<blockquote data-ref="Hebrews 10:24">
And let us consider one another to provoke unto love and to good works.
</blockquote>

---

### 6. 1 Thessalonians 5:11
<blockquote data-ref="1 Thessalonians 5:11">
Wherefore comfort yourselves together, and edify one another, even as also ye do.
</blockquote>

---

### 7. Psalm 121:1–2
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 121:1–2">
I will lift up mine eyes unto the hills, from whence cometh my help. My help cometh from the Lord, which made heaven and earth.
</blockquote>

---

## 🙏 Prayer

God, strengthen my heart when I feel weak and remind me that my help comes from You. Fill me with courage, hope, and peace today. Amen.
