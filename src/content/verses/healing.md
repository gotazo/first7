Seeking healing in body, mind, or spirit? These Bible verses remind us that God restores, renews, and cares deeply for us in every season.

## 💖 7 Bible Verses for Healing

### 1. Jeremiah 30:17
<blockquote data-ref="Jeremiah 30:17">
For I will restore health unto thee, and I will heal thee of thy wounds, saith the Lord.
</blockquote>

---

### 2. Isaiah 53:5
<blockquote data-ref="Isaiah 53:5">
But he was wounded for our transgressions, he was bruised for our iniquities: the chastisement of our peace was upon him; and with his stripes we are healed.
</blockquote>

---

### 3. Psalm 103:2–3
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 103:2–3">
Bless the Lord, O my soul, and forget not all his benefits: Who forgiveth all thine iniquities; who healeth all thy diseases.
</blockquote>

---

### 4. James 5:15
<blockquote data-ref="James 5:15">
And the prayer of faith shall save the sick, and the Lord shall raise him up; and if he have committed sins, they shall be forgiven him.
</blockquote>

---

### 5. Exodus 15:26
<blockquote data-ref="Exodus 15:26">
And said, If thou wilt diligently hearken to the voice of the Lord thy God, and wilt do that which is right in his sight, and wilt give ear to his commandments, and keep all his statutes, I will put none of these diseases upon thee... for I am the Lord that healeth thee.
</blockquote>

---

### 6. Psalm 147:3
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 147:3">
He healeth the broken in heart, and bindeth up their wounds.
</blockquote>

---

### 7. 3 John 1:2
<blockquote data-ref="3 John 1:2">
Beloved, I wish above all things that thou mayest prosper and be in health, even as thy soul prospereth.
</blockquote>

---

## 🙏 Prayer

Lord, bring healing and restoration into every part of my life. Renew my strength, restore my heart, and remind me that You are my healer. Amen.
