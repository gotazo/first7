Feeling alone or unseen? These Bible verses remind us that God is always present, walking beside us and holding us close in every moment.

## 🤍 7 Bible Verses for Loneliness

### 1. Deuteronomy 31:6
<blockquote data-ref="Deuteronomy 31:6">
Be strong and of a good courage, fear not, nor be afraid of them: for the Lord thy God, he it is that doth go with thee; he will not fail thee, nor forsake thee.
</blockquote>

---

### 2. Psalm 68:6
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 68:6">
God setteth the solitary in families: he bringeth out those which are bound with chains: but the rebellious dwell in a dry land.
</blockquote>

---

### 3. Matthew 28:20
<blockquote data-ref="Matthew 28:20">
Teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I have commanded you: and, lo, I am with you alway, even unto the end of the world. Amen.
</blockquote>

---

### 4. Psalm 27:10
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 27:10">
When my father and my mother forsake me, then the Lord will take me up.
</blockquote>

---

### 5. Isaiah 41:10
<blockquote data-ref="Isaiah 41:10">
Fear thou not; for I am with thee: be not dismayed; for I am thy God: I will strengthen thee; yea, I will help thee; yea, I will uphold thee with the right hand of my righteousness.
</blockquote>

---

### 6. John 14:18
<blockquote data-ref="John 14:18">
I will not leave you comfortless: I will come to you.
</blockquote>

---

### 7. Hebrews 13:5
<blockquote data-ref="Hebrews 13:5">
Let your conversation be without covetousness; and be content with such things as ye have: for he hath said, I will never leave thee, nor forsake thee.
</blockquote>

---

## 🙏 Prayer

Lord, when I feel alone, remind me that You are always with me. Surround me with Your presence and fill my heart with peace and comfort. Amen.
