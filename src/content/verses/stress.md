Feeling overwhelmed or under pressure? These Bible verses remind us that God invites us to rest in Him, casting every burden and finding true peace in His presence.

## 🌿 7 Bible Verses for Stress

### 1. Matthew 11:28
<blockquote data-ref="Matthew 11:28">
Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest.
</blockquote>

---

### 2. Philippians 4:6
<blockquote data-ref="Philippians 4:6">
Be careful for nothing; but in every thing by prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be made known unto God.
</blockquote>

---

### 3. Psalm 55:22
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 55:22">
Cast thy burden upon the Lord, and he shall sustain thee: he shall never suffer the righteous to be moved.
</blockquote>

---

### 4. John 14:27
<blockquote data-ref="John 14:27">
Peace I leave with you, my peace I give unto you: not as the world giveth, give I unto you. Let not your heart be troubled, neither let it be afraid.
</blockquote>

---

### 5. Psalm 46:10
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 46:10">
Be still, and know that I am God: I will be exalted among the heathen, I will be exalted in the earth.
</blockquote>

---

### 6. Isaiah 26:3
<blockquote data-ref="Isaiah 26:3">
Thou wilt keep him in perfect peace, whose mind is stayed on thee: because he trusteth in thee.
</blockquote>

---

### 7. 1 Peter 5:7
<blockquote data-ref="1 Peter 5:7">
Casting all your care upon him; for he careth for you.
</blockquote>

---

## 🙏 Prayer

God, in the middle of my stress, help me to rest in You. Calm my thoughts, steady my heart, and remind me that You are in control. Amen.
