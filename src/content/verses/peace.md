Feeling restless or overwhelmed? These Bible verses remind us that God gives a peace that surpasses understanding and guards our hearts in every situation.

## 🕊️ 7 Bible Verses for Peace

### 1. John 14:27
<blockquote data-ref="John 14:27">
Peace I leave with you, my peace I give unto you: not as the world giveth, give I unto you. Let not your heart be troubled, neither let it be afraid.
</blockquote>

---

### 2. Philippians 4:7
<blockquote data-ref="Philippians 4:7">
And the peace of God, which passeth all understanding, shall keep your hearts and minds through Christ Jesus.
</blockquote>

---

### 3. Isaiah 26:3
<blockquote data-ref="Isaiah 26:3">
Thou wilt keep him in perfect peace, whose mind is stayed on thee: because he trusteth in thee.
</blockquote>

---

### 4. Colossians 3:15
<blockquote data-ref="Colossians 3:15">
And let the peace of God rule in your hearts, to the which also ye are called in one body; and be ye thankful.
</blockquote>

---

### 5. Psalm 29:11
<blockquote data-ref="Psalm 29:11">
The Lord will give strength unto his people; the Lord will bless his people with peace.
</blockquote>

---

### 6. Romans 15:13
<blockquote data-ref="Romans 15:13">
Now the God of hope fill you with all joy and peace in believing, that ye may abound in hope, through the power of the Holy Ghost.
</blockquote>

---

### 7. 2 Thessalonians 3:16
<blockquote data-ref="2 Thessalonians 3:16">
Now the Lord of peace himself give you peace always by all means. The Lord be with you all.
</blockquote>

---

## 🙏 Prayer

Lord, fill me with Your peace that calms every fear and quiets every anxious thought. Help me rest in Your presence today. Amen.
