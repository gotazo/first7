title: You are not alone in this
date: 2026-04-03

There are moments when everything feels quiet.

Like no one really understands.

Like you’re carrying things by yourself.

---

### Pause

What have you been carrying alone?

---

God has not stepped away.

Even in the quiet,
He is still near.

You are not alone in this.
