title: Today is enough
date: 2026-04-04
excerpt: You don’t have to figure out everything today
---

I keep thinking about tomorrow.

What could go wrong.
What I need to prepare for.

But it pulls me away from today.

---

### Pause

Can you come back to this moment?

---

Maybe today is enough.

I don’t need to solve everything.

I can just take today as it is,
with God here.
