---
title: Slowing down, even briefly
date: 2026-04-02
excerpt: You don’t have to rush through everything
---

I’ve been moving too fast lately.

From one thought to another.
From one task to the next.

Not really present.

---

### Pause

What would it look like to slow down,
even for a moment?

---

Maybe I don’t need to hurry through today.

Maybe I can take one step at a time.

And trust that God is not rushing me.
