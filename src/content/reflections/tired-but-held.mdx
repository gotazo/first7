---
title: Tired, but still held
date: 2026-04-05
excerpt: You don’t have to be strong all the time
---

I didn’t realize how tired I’ve been.

Not just physically,
but inside.

Trying to hold everything together.

---

### Pause

You don’t have to carry everything right now.

---

Even in your tiredness,
you are still held.

God is not asking you to be strong.

Just to come.
