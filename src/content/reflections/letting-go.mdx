---
title: Letting go, even a little
date: 2026-04-01
excerpt: Sometimes we don’t need to figure everything out
---

I didn’t realize how tightly I’ve been holding onto things.

Even small things.

Trying to make sure everything goes right.

Trying to stay ahead.

It’s tiring.

---

### Pause

What are you holding too tightly?

---

Maybe I don’t need to solve everything today.

Maybe I can let go,
even just a little.

And trust that God is already there.
