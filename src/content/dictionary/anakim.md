## Basic Meaning

Anakim refers to a people group mentioned
in the Old Testament.

They are described as people of great stature
and are associated with the descendants of Anak.

---

## Biblical Usage

The Anakim appear in passages concerning
the land of Canaan and the Israelite conquest.

References to the Anakim occur in Numbers,
Deuteronomy, and Joshua.
