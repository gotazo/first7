## Basic Meaning

A disciple is a follower,
learner, or student.

The word is associated with learning,
following, and obedience.

---

## Biblical Usage

The term disciple appears frequently
in the Gospels and Acts.

Jesus called disciples to follow Him,
learn from Him, and continue His work.
