## Basic Meaning

A calling is an invitation, summons,
vocation, assignment, or purpose
given to a person or group.

The term may be used in religious,
professional, or personal contexts.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word calling appears throughout Scripture
in connection with individuals, nations,
service, responsibilities, and divine purposes.

References occur in both the Old
and New Testaments.
