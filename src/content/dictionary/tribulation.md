## Basic Meaning

Tribulation refers to trouble,
distress, suffering, or affliction.

The word is used to describe
difficult circumstances or hardships.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word tribulation appears throughout Scripture
in teachings, letters,
prophecy, and Revelation.

References to tribulation occur in both
the Gospels and the epistles.
