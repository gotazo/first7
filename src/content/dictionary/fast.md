## Basic Meaning

Fast can describe something that
moves quickly or happens rapidly.

The term can also refer to a period
of abstaining from food, drink,
or certain activities for a specific
purpose.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word fast appears throughout
Scripture in connection with
abstaining from food, prayer,
and religious observance.

The word is also used in ordinary
language to describe speed,
quickness, or rapid movement.
