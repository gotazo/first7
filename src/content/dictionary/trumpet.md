## Basic Meaning

A trumpet is a wind instrument used for signaling,
announcements, ceremonies, or music.

Trumpets have been used throughout history
for communication, celebration, and public gatherings.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word trumpet appears throughout Scripture
in historical accounts, worship, prophecy,
and Revelation.

References to trumpets occur in both the Old Testament
and the New Testament.
