## Basic Meaning

A temple is a building or structure dedicated
to religious worship.

Temples have been used throughout history
by various peoples and cultures.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word temple appears throughout Scripture
in historical accounts, prophetic passages,
and the New Testament.

References to the temple occur in connection
with worship, prayer, service, and Jerusalem.
