## Basic Meaning

A blessing is a benefit, favor,
gift, advantage, or expression
of goodwill toward a person,
group, or situation.

The term may refer to spoken words,
material benefits, opportunities,
or positive outcomes.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word blessing appears throughout
Scripture in connection with people,
families, nations, possessions,
covenants, and spoken declarations.

References occur from Genesis
through Revelation.
