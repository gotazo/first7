## Basic Meaning

Judgment is the act of making a decision,
evaluation, or determination concerning a matter.

The word may refer to a legal decision,
a verdict, or an assessment of actions.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word judgment appears throughout Scripture
in historical narratives, laws,
prophecy, teachings, and Revelation.

References to judgment occur in both
the Old Testament and the New Testament.
