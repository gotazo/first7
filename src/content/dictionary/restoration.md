## Basic Meaning

Restoration is the act of returning something
to a previous condition, position, function,
or state.

The term may refer to rebuilding,
repairing, renewing, or recovering what was lost.

---

## Biblical Usage

Restoration appears throughout Scripture in
connection with people, cities, nations,
relationships, and conditions.

References occur in historical books,
prophecy, the Psalms, and the New Testament.
