## Basic Meaning

Salvation refers to deliverance,
rescue, or preservation from danger,
harm, or loss.

The word can describe being rescued
from a difficult or threatening situation.

---

## Biblical Usage

Salvation appears throughout Scripture
in connection with God's deliverance,
help, and redemption.

References to salvation occur in historical,
poetic, prophetic, and teaching passages.
