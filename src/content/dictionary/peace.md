## Basic Meaning

Peace refers to a state of calm,
harmony, or freedom from conflict.

The word is associated with tranquility
and well-being.

---

## Biblical Usage

Peace appears throughout Scripture
in connection with God,
relationships, and spiritual well-being.

References to peace occur in poetic,
prophetic, and teaching passages.
