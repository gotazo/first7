## Basic Meaning

Bondage is a condition of slavery,
captivity, restraint, or subjection.

The term may describe physical,
social, legal, or personal limitations
on freedom.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word bondage appears throughout
Scripture in historical narratives,
poetry, prophecy, and teachings.
