## Basic Meaning

A priest is a person appointed
to perform sacred duties and religious service.

The word is associated with worship,
sacrifice, and ministry.

---

## Biblical Usage

Priests appear throughout Scripture
in connection with worship,
sacrifice, and the temple.

References to priests occur in both
the Old and New Testaments.
