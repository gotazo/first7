## Basic Meaning

A bride is a woman who is about to be married
or has recently been married.

The word is commonly used in connection with
marriage ceremonies and family relationships.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word bride appears in poetry, prophecy,
parables, and Revelation.

References to a bride occur in both the Old
and New Testaments.
