## Basic Meaning

A promise is a declaration or assurance
that something will happen or be fulfilled.

Promises are commonly associated
with commitment and expectation.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word promise appears throughout Scripture
in connection with God's faithfulness and His word.

References to promises occur in historical,
prophetic, and teaching passages.
