## Meaning

An abomination is something regarded as detestable, hateful, or greatly offensive.

In the Bible, the term is used to describe things that are presented as detestable before God.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word "abomination" appears throughout both the Old and New Testaments.

It is used in connection with various actions, practices, objects, attitudes, and forms of worship described in Scripture as detestable.

The specific context determines how the term is used in each passage.
