## Basic Meaning

An abomination is something regarded
with strong disgust, hatred, rejection,
or intense disapproval.

The term is used for actions, practices,
objects, or behaviors considered
detestable or offensive.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word abomination appears throughout
Scripture in laws, historical accounts,
wisdom literature, prophetic writings,
and teachings.
