## Basic Meaning

Discernment is the ability to recognize,
distinguish, evaluate, or judge between
different ideas, actions, conditions, or situations.

The term is commonly associated with perception,
understanding, and sound judgment.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word discernment appears throughout Scripture
in connection with wisdom, judgment,
understanding, and decision-making.

References occur in wisdom literature,
historical narratives, and New Testament teachings.
