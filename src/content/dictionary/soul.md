## Basic Meaning

The soul is commonly understood as the immaterial
aspect of a living being associated with life,
identity, consciousness, and personal existence.

The term has been used in religious,
philosophical, and cultural contexts throughout history.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word soul appears throughout Scripture in
connection with life, emotions, desires,
strength, and personal identity.

References occur in historical books, poetry,
prophecy, the Gospels, and the epistles.
