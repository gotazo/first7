## Basic Meaning

Heaven may refer to the sky,
the visible expanse above the earth,
outer space, or a heavenly realm.

The term is used in both physical
and spiritual contexts.

---

## Biblical Usage

Heaven appears throughout Scripture
in creation accounts, worship,
prayer, prophecy, visions,
and apocalyptic literature.

References occur from Genesis
through Revelation.
