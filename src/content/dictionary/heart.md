## Basic Meaning

The heart is the organ that circulates blood
through the body and is also commonly used
to describe the center of thought, emotion,
desire, or intention.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word heart appears throughout Scripture
in both its physical sense and as a description
of a person's inner thoughts, desires,
or intentions.
