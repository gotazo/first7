## Basic Meaning

Wrath refers to intense anger,
indignation, or strong displeasure.

The word is often used to describe
a forceful emotional response.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word wrath appears throughout Scripture
in historical narratives, poetry,
prophecy, teachings, and Revelation.

References to wrath occur in both
the Old Testament and the New Testament.
