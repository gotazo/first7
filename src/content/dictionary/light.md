## Basic Meaning

Light is the natural agent that makes
vision possible and illuminates darkness.

The term may refer to physical light,
brightness, illumination, or radiance.

---

## Biblical Usage

Light appears throughout Scripture
in creation accounts, worship imagery,
visions, prophecy, and teachings.

References occur from Genesis
through Revelation.
