## Basic Meaning

Exhortation is encouragement,
instruction, advice, or an appeal
urging someone toward a particular
course of action.

The term is often associated with
guidance, motivation, and counsel.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word exhortation appears in
teachings, letters, and public
addresses throughout Scripture.
