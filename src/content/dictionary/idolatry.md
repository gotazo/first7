## Basic Meaning

Idolatry is the worship,
veneration, devotion,
or excessive reverence given
to an idol, image, object,
or created thing.

The term has been used throughout
history in religious and cultural contexts.

---

## Biblical Usage

Idolatry appears throughout Scripture
in connection with images, statues,
objects of worship, religious practices,
and national customs.

References occur in historical books,
prophetic writings, and the New Testament.
