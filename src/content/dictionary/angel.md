## Meaning

An angel is a spiritual being who serves as a messenger or servant of God.

Throughout the Bible, angels are associated with delivering God's messages, carrying out His commands, ministering to people, and worshipping Him.

---

## Biblical Usage

Angels appear throughout both the Old and New Testaments.

They are presented as God's servants who announce events, provide guidance, offer protection, execute judgment, minister to individuals, and worship before God's throne.
