## Basic Meaning

An angel is a messenger.

The word is used throughout Scripture
for beings that deliver messages,
carry out assignments, or appear in visions.

---

## Biblical Usage

Angels appear throughout Scripture
in historical accounts, prophecy,
the Gospels, and Revelation.

References to angels occur in both
the Old Testament and the New Testament.
