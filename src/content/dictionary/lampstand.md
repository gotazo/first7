## Basic Meaning

A lampstand is a stand or support used for holding lamps.

Lampstands have been used to provide light in homes,
public places, and religious settings.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word lampstand appears throughout Scripture
in connection with the tabernacle, visions,
teachings of Jesus, and Revelation.

References to lampstands occur in Exodus,
Zechariah, the Gospels, and Revelation.
