## Basic Meaning

A redeemer is one who rescues,
delivers, restores, or purchases back.

The word is associated with recovery,
restoration, and deliverance.

---

## Biblical Usage

Scripture frequently refers to God
as Redeemer.

The concept also appears in laws
concerning kinsman redemption.
