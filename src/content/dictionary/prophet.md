## Basic Meaning

A prophet is a person who speaks,
declares, or delivers a message
on behalf of another.

The term is commonly used for individuals who are believed
to communicate messages from a deity.

---

## Biblical Usage

Prophets appear throughout Scripture
in historical narratives, poetry,
prophecy, and the New Testament.

References to prophets occur in both
the Old Testament and the New Testament.
