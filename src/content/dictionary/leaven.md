## Basic Meaning

Leaven is a substance added to dough
that causes it to rise and expand.

Leaven has been used in baking throughout history
to produce bread and other baked foods.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word leaven appears throughout Scripture
in laws, teachings, parables,
and New Testament letters.

References to leaven occur in both
the Old Testament and the New Testament.
