## Basic Meaning

A conscience is an inner awareness
that evaluates actions, conduct,
choices, and decisions.

It is commonly associated with a
person's sense of right and wrong.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word conscience appears in
teachings and letters concerning
conduct, behavior, and personal
responsibility.
