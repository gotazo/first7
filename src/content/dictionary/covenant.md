## Basic Meaning

A covenant is a formal agreement,
promise, or arrangement between parties.

Covenants have been used throughout history
to establish commitments and relationships.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word covenant appears throughout Scripture
in connection with God's promises and agreements.

References to covenants occur in both
the Old Testament and the New Testament.
