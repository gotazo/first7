## Basic Meaning

Faith refers to trust, confidence,
or belief in a person, statement,
or course of action.

The word is commonly associated with
reliance and confidence.

---

## Biblical Usage

Faith appears throughout Scripture
in connection with trust in God,
His promises, and His word.

References to faith occur in historical,
prophetic, and teaching passages.
