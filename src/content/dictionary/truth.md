## Basic Meaning

Truth refers to what is true,
accurate, or in accordance
with reality.

The word is associated with honesty,
reliability, and correctness.

---

## Biblical Usage

Truth appears throughout Scripture
in connection with God's word,
instruction, and character.

References to truth occur in historical,
poetic, prophetic, and teaching passages.
