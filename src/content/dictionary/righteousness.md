## Basic Meaning

Righteousness refers to the quality
of being morally right, just,
or upright.

The word is associated with right conduct,
justice, and integrity.

---

## Biblical Usage

Righteousness appears throughout Scripture
in connection with faith, conduct,
justice, and God's character.

References to righteousness occur in historical,
poetic, prophetic, and teaching passages.
