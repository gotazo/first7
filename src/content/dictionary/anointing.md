## Meaning

Anointing is the act of applying oil or ointment to a person or object.

The practice has been used for ceremonial, cultural, medicinal, and symbolic purposes.

---

## Biblical Usage

Throughout Scripture, anointing is associated with the application of oil to people or objects.

It is used in connection with priests, kings, sacred objects, healing, hospitality, and setting apart people or things for a particular purpose.
