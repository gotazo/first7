## Basic Meaning

Anointing is the act of applying oil,
ointment, or another substance to a
person, object, or place.

The practice has been used for
ceremonial, cultural, medicinal,
and symbolic purposes.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word anointing appears throughout
Scripture in connection with oil,
people, objects, ceremonies,
and religious practices.
