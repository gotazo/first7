## Basic Meaning

Authority is the power, right,
or responsibility to direct,
govern, command, or make decisions.

Authority may belong to individuals,
offices, governments, or institutions.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word authority appears throughout
Scripture in connection with rulers,
leaders, governments, and positions
of responsibility.
