## Basic Meaning

Apostasy refers to a departure from truth,
faith, or previously held beliefs.

The word is associated with abandonment,
rebellion, and turning away.

---

## Biblical Usage

Scripture contains warnings concerning apostasy,
deception, and spiritual decline.

References to apostasy often appear in passages
concerning the last days and false teaching.
