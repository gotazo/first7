## Meaning

Apostasy is the abandonment or turning away from a previously held faith, belief, or commitment.

Throughout Scripture, the word is used in connection with turning away from God, His truth, or the faith.

---

## Biblical Usage

Apostasy appears in both the Old and New Testaments.

It is associated with departing from God's ways, forsaking His covenant, embracing false worship, or turning away from the faith that had been received.
