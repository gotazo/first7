## Basic Meaning

A seal is a mark, stamp, sign, or device used
to authenticate, secure, identify, or close something.

Seals have been used throughout history on documents,
letters, containers, and official records.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word seal appears throughout Scripture
in connection with documents, authority,
identification, and visions.

References to seals occur in historical passages,
prophecy, and Revelation.
