## Basic Meaning

A scroll is a roll of written material
used for recording, preserving,
and reading information.

Before the widespread use of bound books,
scrolls were a common form of written record.

---

## Biblical Usage

Scrolls appear throughout Scripture
in historical accounts, prophetic visions,
and Revelation.

References to scrolls occur in both
the Old Testament and the New Testament.
