## Basic Meaning

A mark is a sign, symbol, impression,
or visible indication placed upon a person,
object, or surface.

Marks may be used for identification,
recognition, ownership, or distinction.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word mark appears in several passages
throughout Scripture.

References to marks occur in historical passages,
visions, and Revelation.
