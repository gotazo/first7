## Basic Meaning

Holiness refers to the quality of being set apart,
pure, or dedicated to a special purpose.

The word is often associated with moral purity
and sacredness.

---

## Biblical Usage

Holiness appears throughout Scripture
in connection with God, worship,
conduct, and devotion.

References to holiness occur in both
the Old Testament and the New Testament.
