## Basic Meaning

Deception is the act of causing
someone to believe something that
is not true.

It may involve misleading statements,
false appearances, concealment,
or dishonesty.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word deception appears throughout
Scripture in historical narratives,
wisdom literature, prophecy,
and teachings.
