## Basic Meaning

An apostle is a person sent
as a messenger or representative.

The word is associated with authority,
mission, and service.

---

## Biblical Usage

The term apostle appears throughout
the New Testament.

Scripture frequently uses the word
for those sent to proclaim and serve.
