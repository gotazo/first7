## Meaning

An apostle is a person sent with a mission or commission.

Throughout the New Testament, the term is used for individuals who were sent with authority to carry out a particular work or ministry.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word "apostle" appears in the New Testament.

It is used for the twelve chosen by Jesus, for Paul, and for others who were sent to carry out God's work. The term is associated with preaching the gospel, teaching, planting churches, and serving in the ministry entrusted to them.
