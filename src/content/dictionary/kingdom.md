## Basic Meaning

A kingdom is a territory, realm, or community
ruled by a king or other sovereign authority.

Kingdoms have existed throughout history as organized
political and governmental entities.

---

## Biblical Usage

The word kingdom appears throughout Scripture
in historical narratives, teachings,
parables, prophecy, and Revelation.

References to kingdoms occur in both
the Old Testament and the New Testament.
