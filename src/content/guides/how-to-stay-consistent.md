## How to Stay Consistent

Consistency is not about discipline alone.

It’s about **returning to God daily**.

Not perfectly.  
But faithfully.

---

## 🕊️ Let It Be Simple

You don’t need long sessions.

Just:

- read  
- reflect  
- respond  

That’s enough.

---

## 🔁 Come Back Each Day

Some days will feel meaningful.

Some won’t.

Still come back.

God meets you in both.

---

## 📉 When You Feel Unmotivated

Don’t wait for a feeling.

Start small.

Even one verse, one thought, one prayer.

---

## ❤️ Focus on Returning

You don’t need to never miss.

You just need to return.

That’s where growth happens.

---

## 🙏 A Short Prayer

God, help me come back to You each day.  
Even when I feel distracted or tired.  
Build a steady rhythm in my life.  
Amen.
