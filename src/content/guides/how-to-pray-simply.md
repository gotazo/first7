## How to Pray Simply

Prayer is not a ritual.

It is **communion with God**.

It’s not about saying perfect words —  
it’s about being present with Him.

---

## 🕊️ Prayer Is Being With God

You don’t need to perform.

You don’t need to impress.

You are simply coming to God as you are.

Just like you would sit with someone you trust.

---

## 💬 You Can Say Anything

You can bring everything to God.

- your worries  
- your thoughts  
- your frustrations  
- your questions  

You don’t need to filter it.

You can even say:

> “God, I don’t understand what I’m feeling.”

That is real prayer.

---

## ❤️ Be Honest, Not Perfect

God is not looking for perfect words.

He is looking for your heart.

You can:

- vent  
- question  
- cry  
- sit in silence  

All of that is part of prayer.

---

## ✍️ Prayer Can Be Simple

You don’t need long prayers.

Even a few words matter:

- “Help me”  
- “I feel overwhelmed”  
- “Be with me”  

That’s enough.

---

## 🧩 When You Don’t Know What to Say

Just start somewhere.

You can:

- repeat a verse  
- speak one sentence  
- sit quietly  

God is still with you.

---

## ⏱️ You Don’t Need a Perfect Moment

Prayer doesn’t need a schedule.

You can pray:

- in the morning  
- in the middle of your day  
- in a quiet moment  

It’s about connection, not timing.

---

## 🔁 Stay With God, Even Briefly

You don’t need long time.

Even a small moment of real connection matters.

Over time, those moments grow deeper.

---

## 💛 Prayer Is a Relationship

You are not speaking into silence.

God hears you.

God sees you.

God is with you.

---

## ❤️ A Simple Way to Start

Right now, try this:

- tell God what you feel  
- share what’s on your mind  
- ask for what you need  

Just speak honestly.

---

## 🙏 A Short Prayer

God, thank You that I can come to You as I am.  
Help me be honest and open with You.  
Teach me to stay close to You in every moment.  
Amen.
