## When You Feel Anxious

Anxiety can feel overwhelming.

But you are not alone in it.

God is with you in this moment.

---

## 🕊️ Bring It to God

You don’t need to fix everything.

Just say:

> “God, I feel anxious.”

---

## ✍️ Let It Out

Write what you’re feeling.

This helps you process what’s inside.

---

## 🔁 Return to Trust

You don’t have to control everything.

You can trust God with what you don’t understand.

---

## ❤️ Take One Small Step

You don’t need full clarity.

Just take one step forward.

---

## 🙏 A Short Prayer

God, You see my thoughts and my fears.  
Help me trust You and find peace in You.  
Guide me step by step.  
Amen.
