## What to Do If You Miss a Day

You will miss days.

That doesn’t separate you from God.

You can always come back.

---

## 🕊️ There Is No Distance With God

God doesn’t move away when you miss a day.

He is still there.

Ready to meet you again.

---

## 🔁 Just Return

Don’t restart.

Don’t overthink.

Just continue.

---

## 💬 Let Go of Guilt

Guilt makes things heavier.

Grace brings you back.

---

## ❤️ Keep Moving Forward

Even a small step today matters more than a perfect plan.

---

## 🙏 A Short Prayer

God, thank You that I can always come back.  
Help me release guilt and continue forward.  
Keep me close to You each day.  
Amen.
