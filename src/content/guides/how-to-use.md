## Welcome to the 7-Day Journey

This plan is meant to be practiced, reflected on, and lived out one day at a time.

Over the next 7 days, you will learn how to:

- release control
- trust God more deeply
- find peace even in uncertainty

## How to Use This Plan

Each day is designed to be simple and focused.

### 1. Read the Truth

Start with the verse and short reflection.

Do not rush this part. Let it sit with you for a moment.

### 2. Write Your Reflection

Use the reflection space to write honestly about:

- what you are feeling
- what you are struggling with
- what stood out to you

Your reflections are saved automatically on your device.

### 3. Take One Small Step

Each day includes a simple action.

Even one small step can build real change.

### 4. Pray Simply

End each day with a short prayer.

It does not have to be perfect. Just be honest.

## Your Journal

Everything you write is saved automatically.

You can view your reflections anytime in your journal.

## Download Your Journal

You can download your reflections as a PDF anytime.

## Stay Consistent

You do not need perfection.

Just come back each day.

Consistency builds clarity.

## A Final Thought

You are not alone.

Take it one day at a time.
