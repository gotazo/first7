## 💪 Prayers for Strength

<div class="prayer" data-title="Strength" data-ref="Philippians 4:13">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Lord, when I feel weak, be my strength. Help me stand firm and continue, even when it feels difficult.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Endurance" data-ref="Isaiah 40:31">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Father, give me endurance to face what lies ahead. Strengthen my spirit so I do not give up.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Courage" data-ref="Joshua 1:9">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    God, replace my fear with courage. Help me walk boldly, knowing You are with me.
  </p>

</div>
