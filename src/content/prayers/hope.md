## 🌅 Prayers for Hope

<div class="prayer" data-title="Future and Hope" data-ref="Jeremiah 29:11">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Lord, when I feel uncertain about the future, remind me that Your plans are good. Fill my heart with peace and help me trust that You are leading me toward hope and purpose.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Abound in Hope" data-ref="Romans 15:13">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Father, fill me with joy and peace as I place my trust in You. Let Your Spirit strengthen my heart so I may overflow with hope even in difficult seasons.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="My Hope Is in Thee" data-ref="Psalm 39:7">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    God, in every waiting season, help me keep my eyes on You. When I feel discouraged or weary, remind me that my true hope is found in Your faithfulness.
  </p>

</div>
