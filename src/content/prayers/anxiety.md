## 🙏 Prayers for Anxiety

<div class="prayer" data-title="Peace" data-ref="1 Peter 5:7">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Lord, calm my anxious heart. When my thoughts race, help me rest in Your presence and trust in You.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Trust" data-ref="Proverbs 3:5">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Father, I give You every worry I carry. Teach me to trust You fully, even when I cannot see the way forward.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Rest" data-ref="Philippians 4:7">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    God, quiet my mind and give me peace that surpasses understanding. Let my soul find rest in You.
  </p>

</div>
