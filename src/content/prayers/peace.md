## 🕊️ Prayers for Peace

<div class="prayer" data-title="Peace" data-ref="John 14:27">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Lord, fill my heart with peace. Remove every worry and replace it with calm assurance.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Stillness" data-ref="Psalm 46:10">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    God, help me be still and trust You. Quiet every storm within me.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Calm" data-ref="Isaiah 26:3">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Father, bring calm to my thoughts and clarity to my mind. Let Your peace guide me.
  </p>

</div>
