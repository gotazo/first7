## 🌿 Prayers Against Worry

<div class="prayer" data-title="One Day at a Time" data-ref="Matthew 6:34">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Lord, help me not to be consumed by worry about tomorrow. Teach me to trust You daily and rest in the peace of knowing that You are already taking care of my future.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Bring Every Care to God" data-ref="Philippians 4:6">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Father, instead of holding onto anxiety, help me bring every concern to You in prayer. Fill my heart with gratitude and peace as I place my needs into Your hands.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Trust Beyond Worry" data-ref="Luke 12:25">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    God, remind me that worry cannot change my circumstances. Help me release fearful thoughts and trust completely in Your wisdom, provision, and care for my life.
  </p>

</div>
