##  Prayers Against Fear

<div class="prayer" data-title="Fear Not" data-ref="Isaiah 41:13">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Lord, when fear overwhelms me, remind me that You are holding my hand. Help me trust in Your strength and rest in Your promise that You will help me through every trial.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Be Strong" data-ref="Joshua 1:9">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Father, give me courage when I feel weak and afraid. Help me stand strong and not be discouraged, knowing that You are with me wherever I go.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Delivered From Fear" data-ref="Psalm 34:4">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    God, I seek You with all my heart. Hear my prayers and calm every anxious thought within me. Deliver me from fear and fill my soul with Your peace.
  </p>

</div>
