## 🌿 Prayers for Stress

<div class="prayer" data-title="Come Unto Me" data-ref="Matthew 11:28">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Lord, I come to You feeling tired and overwhelmed. Help me find rest in Your presence and peace in the middle of every stressful situation I face.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Prayer Instead of Anxiety" data-ref="Philippians 4:6">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Father, teach me to bring every worry and burden to You in prayer. Fill my heart with thanksgiving and calm my anxious thoughts with Your peace.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Cast Your Burden" data-ref="Psalm 55:22">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    God, I place every heavy burden into Your hands today. Sustain me with Your strength and help me stand firm, trusting that You will carry me through.
  </p>

</div>
