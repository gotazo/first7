## 🌟 Prayers for Encouragement

<div class="prayer" data-title="Be Strong and Courageous" data-ref="Joshua 1:9">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Lord, help me be strong and courageous when fear and discouragement try to overcome me. Remind me that You are always with me wherever I go.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Strengthen My Heart" data-ref="Psalm 31:24">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Father, strengthen my heart when I feel weary or uncertain. Fill me with courage and help me continue placing my hope and trust in You.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Power to the Faint" data-ref="Isaiah 40:29">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    God, when I feel weak and exhausted, renew my strength. Give me the power to keep moving forward and help me rest in Your unfailing support.
  </p>

</div>
