## ❤️ Prayers for Love

<div class="prayer" data-title="Kind and Patient Love" data-ref="1 Corinthians 13:4–5">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Lord, teach me to love with patience and kindness. Remove pride, anger, and selfishness from my heart, and help me treat others with grace and compassion each day.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="God’s Great Love" data-ref="John 3:16">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Father, thank You for loving the world so deeply that You gave Your Son for us. Help me live in that love and share Your mercy, hope, and goodness with others.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Love One Another" data-ref="1 John 4:7">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    God, fill my heart with love that comes from You. Help me love others sincerely and walk closely with You, showing kindness and understanding in all that I do.
  </p>

</div>
