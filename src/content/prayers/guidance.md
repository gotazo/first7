## 🧭 Prayers for Guidance

<div class="prayer" data-title="Direction" data-ref="Proverbs 3:6">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Lord, guide my steps and show me the right path. Help me trust Your direction.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Wisdom" data-ref="James 1:5">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Father, give me wisdom in every decision I make. Lead me in truth.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Clarity" data-ref="Proverbs 3:5">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    God, clear my confusion and help me see clearly what I should do next.
  </p>

</div>
