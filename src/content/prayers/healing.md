## ❤️ Prayers for Healing

<div class="prayer" data-title="Healing" data-ref="Psalm 147:3">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Lord, bring healing to my body and my heart. Restore what is broken and renew my strength.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Comfort" data-ref="2 Corinthians 1:3">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    Father, comfort me in my pain. Surround me with Your presence and peace.
  </p>

</div>

<div class="prayer" data-title="Restoration" data-ref="Jeremiah 30:17">

  <p class="prayer-text">
    God, restore my health and renew my spirit. Let Your healing power flow through me.
  </p>

</div>
