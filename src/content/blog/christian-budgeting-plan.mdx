# A Simple Christian Budget  
*A practical way to manage money with faith and clarity*

---

## 💬 My Story

This idea didn’t come from theory—it came from real life.

There were times I would forget to give my tithe. Not because I didn’t care, but because I didn’t have a system.

I also wanted to help others, even with a small income. But it remained more of an intention than something consistent.

Even at home, I would tell my wife that we should set aside money for different purposes. But without structure, we often ended up using it for something else.

That’s when I realized:

👉 The problem wasn’t intention—it was lack of clarity.

So I began thinking:

*What if I could divide my income in a simple and consistent way?*

And that’s how this system started.

I believe every good idea ultimately comes from God.

> “Every good gift and every perfect gift is from above…” — James 1:17

---

## 💡 A Simple 5-Part Budget

This is the structure I now follow:

- **10% Tithe (Giving to Church)** — to honor God  
- **5% Helping Others** — intentional generosity  
- **5% Emergency Fund** — preparing for uncertainty  
- **10% Future Savings** — building stability  (Optional)
- **70% Living** — daily needs  

It’s simple, but powerful.
