Comparison can quietly shape how you see yourself.

You look at others,
and wonder if you’re behind,
or not enough.

But your life is not meant to be measured against someone else’s.

---

### Pause

Where have you been comparing yourself lately?

---

God is writing a story with your life.

Not someone else’s.
