It’s easy to feel unnoticed.

To move through your days wondering if anyone truly sees you,
or understands what you carry.

But God sees you.

Not just the surface,
but everything beneath it.

He sees your thoughts,
your struggles,
your quiet moments.

---

### Pause

Sit with this for a moment.

You are not invisible to God.

---

You are fully known.

And still, fully loved.

## 🙏 A simple step

Take a moment today to be still.

without needing to explain anything.
