You may have learned to measure your worth.

Through performance.
Through success.
Through what others think of you.

But your value is not something you earn.

It is something God gives.

---

### Pause

Where do you usually look to feel “enough”?

---

God’s love is not based on what you do.

It’s based on who He is.

## 🙏 A simple step

Notice when you try to prove yourself today.

Gently remind yourself:

You don’t have to earn what God has already given.
