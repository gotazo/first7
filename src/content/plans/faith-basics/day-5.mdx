## Understanding the Bible

"All Scripture is God-breathed..." - 2 Timothy 3:16

The Bible is how God speaks to us. It guides, teaches, and corrects us.

Reading the Bible is not about knowledge - it's about transformation.

Start small. Stay consistent.

## Reflection
- How often do I read the Bible?
- Do I approach it with an open heart?

## Prayer
God, help me understand Your Word. Speak to me through Scripture.
