## Staying Consistent

"Let us not become weary in doing good..." - Galatians 6:9

Faith is a journey, not a one-time decision.

Consistency matters more than intensity.

Even small daily steps build strong faith over time.

## Reflection
- What habits can I build to stay consistent?
- How can I continue this journey?

## Prayer
God, help me stay consistent. Give me strength to continue growing daily.
