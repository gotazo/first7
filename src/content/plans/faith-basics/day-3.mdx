## What is Salvation?

"For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith..." - Ephesians 2:8

Salvation is a gift from God. It is not something we earn - it is something we receive.

We all fall short, but God offers forgiveness through Jesus.

This means you don't have to be perfect. You only need to trust Him.

## Reflection
- Am I trying to earn God's love?
- Do I truly believe salvation is a gift?

## Prayer
God, thank You for Your grace. Help me to receive Your gift of salvation fully.
