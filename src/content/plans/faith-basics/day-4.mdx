## Learning to Pray

"Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation... present your requests to God." - Philippians 4:6

Prayer is simply talking to God. It doesn't need to be complicated.

You can speak honestly - God already knows your heart.

Prayer builds your relationship with Him.

## Reflection
- Do I talk to God regularly?
- What holds me back from praying?

## Prayer
God, teach me how to pray. Help me to speak to You honestly and often.
