## Living Out Your Faith

"Do not merely listen to the word... Do what it says." - James 1:22

Faith is not just belief - it is action.

Your daily choices reflect your faith.

Start with small steps: kindness, honesty, love.

## Reflection
- Does my life reflect my faith?
- What is one action I can take today?

## Prayer
God, help me live out my faith. Let my actions reflect Your truth.
