## Who is God?

"In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth." - Genesis 1:1

God is not just a concept. He is the Creator of everything. Before anything existed, God was there. He is eternal, powerful, and sovereign over all creation.

But God is not distant. He is personal. He created you with purpose and knows you deeply.

Today, begin by recognizing this truth: **God is real, and He is present in your life.**
