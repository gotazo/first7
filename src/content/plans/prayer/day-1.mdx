Many people feel unsure about prayer.

What do I say?
Am I doing it right?
Do I need the right words?

But prayer doesn’t begin with perfect words.

It begins with coming as you are.

---

### Pause

Take a breath.

You don’t need to prepare anything.

---

God is not waiting for you to sound right.

He is simply inviting you to come.

## 🙏 A simple step

Just honestly.
