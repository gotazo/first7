Overthinking often comes from control.

If you think enough,
you might prevent something from going wrong.

But thinking cannot control life.

God already holds what you’re trying to manage.

---

### Pause

What are you trying to control right now?

---

You can release it.

Not all at once,
but one small step at a time.

---

<ReflectionBox plan="overthinking" day={3} />

---

## 🙏 A simple step

Notice when you try to control something today.

Instead of holding tighter,
choose to let go.
