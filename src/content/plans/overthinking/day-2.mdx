Your thoughts can move fast.

Jumping from one possibility to another,
trying to stay ahead of everything.

But peace is not found in rushing.

It’s found in stillness.

---

### Pause

Be still for a moment.

Even just for a few seconds.

---

God is not in a hurry.

And you don’t need to be either.

## 🙏 A simple step

Slow one moment down today.

Pause before reacting.
Pause before thinking ahead.

Just be present.
