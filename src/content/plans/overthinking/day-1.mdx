Overthinking often feels like responsibility.

If you think long enough,
analyze deeply enough,
maybe you’ll find the right answer.

But not everything is yours to solve.

Some things are meant to be trusted,
not figured out.

---

### Pause

Take a breath.

What are you trying to figure out right now?

---

God is not waiting for you to have all the answers.

He is inviting you to trust Him,
even in the unknown.

## 🙏 A simple step

Name one thing you’ve been trying to figure out.

Give it to God,
even if your mind wants to take it back.
