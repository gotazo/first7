Anxiety often begins quietly.

A thought.
A concern.
A sense that everything depends on you.

You try to hold things together.
To control outcomes.
To make sure nothing goes wrong.

But God is not asking you to carry what He already holds.

He is already present.
Already working.
Already in control.

---

### Pause

Take a moment.

Breathe.

What are you trying to hold onto right now?

---

Trust doesn’t mean you understand everything.

It simply means you choose to rest in who God is,
even when things feel uncertain.

You don’t have to figure everything out today.

You can let go, little by little.

## 🙏 A simple step

Bring one specific thing to God.

You don’t need perfect words.

Just be honest.

And when you’ve given it to Him,
try not to carry it again.
