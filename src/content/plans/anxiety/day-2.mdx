God does not ask you to hold everything together.

He invites you to release it.

Anxiety often builds when we keep carrying things alone — thoughts, fears, pressure, expectations.

But God cares about what concerns you.

You don’t need to carry it anymore.

You can give it to Him.

## 🎯 Today’s Step

Take a moment and name one burden you’re carrying.

Say it honestly to God.

Then consciously release it — and don’t take it back.
