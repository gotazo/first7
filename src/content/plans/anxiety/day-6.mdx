Anxiety can feel isolating.

Like no one understands.

Like you’re alone in it.

But God is near — especially when you feel overwhelmed.

He is not distant from your pain.

He is present in it.

Even when you don’t feel Him, He is there.

## 🎯 Today’s Step

“God, I know You are here.”

Stay there without rushing.
