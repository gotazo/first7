Life does not always become calm.

But God gives peace even in the middle of chaos.

His peace is not based on what’s happening around you.

It comes from His presence within you.

You don’t have to wait for everything to be perfect to feel peace.

Peace is available right now.

## 🎯 Today’s Step

“God is with me right now.”

Let that bring you back to peace.
