Anxiety often begins with a thought.

“What if something goes wrong?”

“What if I can’t handle it?”

But not every thought is true.

God invites you to renew your mind — to replace fear with truth.

What you think repeatedly shapes how you feel.

## 🎯 Today’s Step

Notice one anxious thought today.

Replace it with truth:

“God is with me.”  
“I don’t have to fear this.”
