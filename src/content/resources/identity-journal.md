# Know Your Worth in Christ

Your identity is not defined by success, failure, comparison, or the opinions of others.

This journal helps you discover what God says about your value and encourages you to build your confidence on His truth.

Inside you'll find:

- Seven days of Scripture reflection
- Identity-focused journaling prompts
- Personal reflection exercises
- Prayer moments
- Encouragement rooted in God's Word
