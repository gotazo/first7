# Release Control and Find Peace

Anxiety often makes us feel like we have to carry everything ourselves. This 7-day journal is designed to help you slow down, reflect on God's promises, and take one faithful step each day.

Inside you'll find:

- Seven days of guided Scripture reflection
- Gentle journaling prompts
- Simple prayers
- Space to pause and listen to God
- Encouragement rooted in the Bible

Whether you're feeling overwhelmed, worried about the future, or simply need a quiet place to refocus, this journal is here to help you remember that God is with you.
