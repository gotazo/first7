# Build a Daily Prayer Habit

Prayer doesn't have to be complicated.

God invites you to come just as you are, bringing your joys, struggles, questions, and gratitude before Him. This 7-day journal is designed to help you slow down, reflect on Scripture, and develop a simple rhythm of daily prayer.

Inside you'll find:

- Seven days of guided prayer
- Bible verses for daily reflection
- Journaling prompts
- Space to write your prayers
- Encouragement to grow closer to God

Whether you're beginning your prayer journey or rebuilding a daily habit, this journal offers a simple place to start.

> **"Pray without ceasing."** — 1 Thessalonians 5:17 (KJV)
