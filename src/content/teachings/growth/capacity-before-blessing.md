title: Capacity Comes Before Increase

summary: God often expands your character and capacity before releasing greater things.

topics:
  - growth
  - preparation
  - maturity

keywords:
  - capacity
  - increase
  - preparation
  - expansion

scripture:
  ref: "Luke 16:10"
  text: "He that is faithful in that which is least is faithful also in much."

truth: God prepares people internally before increasing them externally.

insight:
  - Greater blessings require greater stability.
  - Capacity determines sustainability.
  - God stretches people before promotion.

application:
  - Be faithful in small things.
  - Let God build your character.
  - Prepare for what you are praying for.

prayer: Lord, expand my capacity for what You want to place in my life.
