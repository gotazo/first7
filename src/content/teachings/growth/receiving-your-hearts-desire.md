title: Receiving the Desires of Your Heart

summary: God gives desires through alignment, flexibility, maturity, and spiritual posture.

topics:
  - desire
  - posture
  - growth
  - faith

keywords:
  - flexibility
  - maturity
  - waiting
  - capacity
  - positioning
  - change

scripture:
  ref: "Psalm 37:4"
  text: "Delight thyself also in the Lord; and he shall give thee the desires of thine heart."

truth: God is willing to give desires, but desires require spiritual posture and capacity.

insight:
  - Every blessing and promise has a required posture.
  - God's promises and personal desires are not always the same thing.
  - Wrong posture can delay what God intended for you.
  - Flexibility is necessary for spiritual growth.
  - Capacity determines what you can sustain.

application:
  - Stay flexible before God.
  - Make room for what God wants to bring.
  - Guard your joy during the process.
  - Keep your eyes on Jesus.
  - Stop carrying old seasons into new ones.

prayer: Father, shape my heart and posture me correctly to receive everything You desire for my life.
