title: Thoughts and Intentions

summary: God examines the thoughts and intentions behind prayer, not just the words spoken.

topics:
  - prayer
  - intention
  - heart

keywords:
  - thoughts
  - motives
  - purity
  - sincerity
  - prayer life

scripture:
  ref: "Hebrews 4:12"
  text: "For the word of God is quick, and powerful, and sharper than any twoedged sword... and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart."

truth: God evaluates the intention behind actions before the action itself.

insight:
  - Wrong intentions can corrupt spiritual actions.
  - Pure intentions matter more than polished words.
  - Thoughts can slowly redirect intentions.
  - God weighs the heart continually.

application:
  - Examine your motives before praying.
  - Ask God to purify your heart.
  - Stop performing spiritually for appearances.
  - Pray with sincerity and honesty.

prayer: Father, purify my thoughts and intentions before You.
