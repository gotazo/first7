title: Why Some Seeds Never Produce

summary: A lack of wisdom, obedience, and understanding can prevent spiritual fruitfulness.

topics:
  - giving
  - discernment

keywords:
  - seed
  - fruitfulness
  - stewardship
  - wisdom
  - obedience
  - discernment

scripture:
  ref: "Hosea 4:6"
  text: "My people are destroyed for lack of knowledge: because thou hast rejected knowledge, I will also reject thee, that thou shalt be no priest to me: seeing thou hast forgotten the law of thy God, I will also forget thy children."

truth: Spiritual fruitfulness requires wisdom, obedience, and understanding.

insight:
  - Not every action produces lasting spiritual fruit.
  - Careless sowing often leads to disappointment.
  - God desires understanding alongside generosity.
  - Obedience and discernment protect spiritual growth.

application:
  - Seek wisdom before major decisions
  - Avoid emotional or careless giving
  - Grow in spiritual discernment
  - Remain teachable before God

prayer: Father, give me wisdom and understanding so my life produces lasting spiritual fruit according to Your will.
