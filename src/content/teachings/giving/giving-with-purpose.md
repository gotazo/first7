title: Giving With Purpose

summary: Biblical giving is not random. God honors intentional and meaningful sacrifice.

topics:
  - giving
  - stewardship

keywords:
  - giving
  - purpose
  - stewardship
  - offering
  - intentional giving
  - sacrifice

scripture:
  ref: "2 Corinthians 9:7"
  text: "Every man according as he purposeth in his heart, so let him give; not grudgingly, or of necessity: for God loveth a cheerful giver."

truth: God values intentional giving that flows from the heart.

insight:
  - Giving in Scripture was often connected to worship and purpose.
  - God looks beyond the amount and examines the posture of the heart.
  - Meaningful giving reflects trust and obedience.
  - Purposeful giving creates spiritual alignment.

application:
  - Give prayerfully instead of emotionally
  - Ask God for wisdom before giving
  - Avoid careless or pressured giving
  - Let generosity reflect worship and trust

prayer: Father, teach me to give with wisdom, sincerity, and purpose. Help my generosity reflect obedience and worship toward You.
