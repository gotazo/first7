  - giving
  - understanding

keywords:
  - understanding
  - giving

scripture:
  ref: "Proverbs 4:7"
  text: "Wisdom is the principal thing; therefore get wisdom: and with all thy getting get understanding."

truth: God calls believers to combine generosity with wisdom and understanding.

insight:
  - Understanding protects believers from confusion and manipulation.
  - Spiritual maturity requires wisdom in every area of stewardship.
  - God honors generosity that flows from discernment.
  - Understanding strengthens faithful obedience.

application:
  - Pursue wisdom before making decisions
  - Study Scripture carefully
  - Pray for discernment
  - Practice thoughtful stewardship

prayer: Father, give me wisdom and understanding so I may walk faithfully and steward every resource according to Your will.
