title: Speak To Your Offering

summary: Giving should be connected with prayer, faith, and intentional surrender before God.

topics:
  - giving
  - faith

keywords:
  - offering
  - prayer
  - faith
  - seed
  - giving
  - surrender

scripture:
  ref: "Ecclesiastes 11:6"
  text: "In the morning sow thy seed, and in the evening withhold not thine hand: for thou knowest not whether shall prosper, either this or that, or whether they both shall be alike good."

truth: Biblical giving involves faith, prayer, and spiritual intention.

insight:
  - Giving is not merely financial activity but spiritual surrender.
  - Scripture often connects sowing with expectation and trust.
  - Prayer helps align giving with God's direction.
  - Intentional giving strengthens faith and dependence on God.

application:
  - Pray before giving
  - Surrender your motives to God
  - Trust God instead of outcomes
  - Give in faith, not fear

prayer: Father, let my giving be rooted in faith and surrender. Teach me to trust You fully as I sow with wisdom and obedience.
