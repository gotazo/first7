---
id: giving-and-vision
title: Giving Reveals Your Vision

summary: The way you give often reveals what matters most in your heart and life.

topics: ["giving", "heart"]
keywords: ["giving", "treasure", "value", "sacrifice", "vision"]

scripture:
  ref: "Matthew 6:21"
  text: "For where your treasure is, there will your heart be also."

truth: The things you are willing to give your time, energy, and resources to often reveal what you truly value most.

insight:
  - Genuine giving usually involves sacrifice and comes from a sincere heart.
  - What you invest in consistently can reveal the direction and priorities of your life.
  - Giving is not only about money, but also about love, kindness, time, and service.

application:
  - Give with purpose and sincerity
  - Reflect on what your daily priorities say about your heart
  - Be willing to help others with both generosity and compassion

prayer: Father, teach me to give with a sincere heart and help my life reflect what truly matters to You.
