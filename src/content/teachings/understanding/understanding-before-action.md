title: Understanding Before Action

summary: Wisdom and understanding help believers act with clarity, discernment, and obedience.

topics:
  - understanding
  - wisdom

keywords:
  - understanding
  - wisdom
  - discernment
  - obedience
  - guidance
  - patience

scripture:
  ref: "Proverbs 4:7"
  text: "Wisdom is the principal thing; therefore get wisdom: and with all thy getting get understanding."

truth: God desires believers to pursue wisdom and understanding before acting.

insight:
  - Rushed decisions often create unnecessary confusion.
  - Wisdom helps believers recognize God's direction.
  - Understanding protects against emotional reactions.
  - Patience allows room for discernment and clarity.

application:
  - Pray before making major decisions
  - Seek wisdom through Scripture
  - Slow down before reacting emotionally
  - Remain teachable and humble

prayer: Father, give me wisdom and understanding so I may walk carefully and make decisions that honor You.
