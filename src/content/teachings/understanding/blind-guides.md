---

title: Blind Guides

summary: Jesus warned against following spiritual leadership that lacks wisdom, truth, and discernment.

topics:
  - understanding
  - leadership

keywords:
  - leadership
  - discernment
  - wisdom
  - truth
  - guidance
  - spiritual growth

scripture:
  ref: "Matthew 15:14"
  text: "Let them alone: they be blind leaders of the blind. And if the blind lead the blind, both shall fall into the ditch."

truth: Spiritual discernment is necessary when receiving guidance and leadership.

insight:
  - Not every voice leads toward truth and wisdom.
  - Discernment protects believers from confusion.
  - Spiritual maturity requires testing what is taught.
  - God calls leaders to humility and truth.

application:
  - Compare teaching with Scripture
  - Grow in spiritual discernment
  - Seek wise and godly counsel
  - Remain grounded in truth

prayer: Father, give me discernment to recognize truth and wisdom so I may walk faithfully according to Your Word.
