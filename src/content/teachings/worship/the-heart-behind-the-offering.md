title: The Heart Behind The Offering

summary: God examines the motives, sincerity, and posture of the heart behind every offering.

topics:
  - worship
  - giving

keywords:
  - offering
  - worship
  - heart
  - sincerity
  - generosity
  - obedience

scripture:
  ref: "1 Samuel 16:7"
  text: "For the LORD seeth not as man seeth; for man looketh on the outward appearance, but the LORD looketh on the heart."

truth: God values the condition of the heart more than outward appearance alone.

insight:
  - Motives matter deeply in worship and giving.
  - God examines sincerity beyond visible actions.
  - Worship should flow from love and devotion.
  - Spiritual maturity develops through humility and honesty.

application:
  - Examine your motives honestly
  - Practice sincere worship and generosity
  - Avoid outward performance without inward devotion
  - Grow in humility before God

prayer: Father, purify my heart and help my worship, generosity, and devotion reflect sincerity and love toward You.
