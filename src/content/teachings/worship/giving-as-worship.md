title: Giving As Worship

summary: Generosity becomes worship when it flows from gratitude, obedience, and love toward God.

topics:
  - worship
  - giving

keywords:
  - giving
  - worship
  - generosity
  - stewardship
  - gratitude
  - obedience

scripture:
  ref: "2 Corinthians 9:7"
  text: "Every man according as he purposeth in his heart, so let him give; not grudgingly, or of necessity: for God loveth a cheerful giver."

truth: Biblical generosity reflects worship, gratitude, and trust in God.

insight:
  - Giving can become an act of worship and surrender.
  - God values cheerful and willing generosity.
  - Gratitude transforms stewardship into worship.
  - Worship reflects trust in God's provision and faithfulness.

application:
  - Give with gratitude and sincerity
  - Practice joyful generosity
  - Trust God with your resources
  - Let worship influence your stewardship

prayer: Father, let my generosity become worship that reflects gratitude, obedience, and trust in You.
