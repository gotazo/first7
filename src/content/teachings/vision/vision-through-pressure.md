keywords:

scripture:
  ref: "Proverbs 29:18"
  text: "Where there is no vision, the people perish: but he that keepeth the law, happy is he."

truth: Sometimes God allows pressure in our lives, not to destroy us, but to help us grow and see things differently.

insight:
  - When familiar things begin to change, God may be leading you into a new season of growth.
  - Difficult moments can reveal areas where we depend too much on comfort or fear.
  - Spiritual growth often begins during uncomfortable and challenging seasons.

application:
  - Stay calm and trust God during difficult seasons
  - Ask God what He wants to teach you through your struggles
  - Remain willing to grow, change, and trust His direction

prayer: Father, help me grow through every difficult season and open my eyes to see beyond my current situation.
