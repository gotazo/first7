title: Three Types of Vision

summary: There are natural, mental, and spiritual levels of vision.

topics:
  - vision
  - discernment

keywords:
  - vision
  - discernment
  - faith
  - sight
  - spiritual vision

scripture:
  ref: "2 Corinthians 5:7"
  text: "For we walk by faith, not by sight:"

truth: Not all vision comes from God.

insight:
  - Natural vision is based on what you physically see.
  - Mental vision is shaped by imagination and personal expectation.
  - Spiritual vision comes from what God reveals through faith.
  - Living only by sight or emotion limits spiritual growth.

application:
  - Do not rely on feelings alone
  - Seek God's perspective through prayer and Scripture
  - Learn to walk by faith instead of appearances

prayer: Lord, help me see through Your perspective and walk by faith.
