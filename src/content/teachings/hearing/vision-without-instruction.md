title: Vision Without Instruction

summary: Seeing without hearing can lead to confusion, fear, and wrong direction.

topics:
  - hearing
  - guidance
  - discernment

keywords:
  - hearing God
  - spiritual ears
  - instruction
  - visions
  - dreams
  - discernment

scripture:
  ref: "Job 33:15-16"
  text: "In a dream, in a vision of the night, when deep sleep falleth upon men, in slumberings upon the bed; Then he openeth the ears of men, and sealeth their instruction."

truth: God does not only show visions. He gives instruction through spiritual hearing.

insight:
  - Spiritual ears are necessary to understand what God is revealing.
  - Many people see visions or dreams but miss their meaning because they do not hear God's instruction.
  - Rebellion often begins with the inability to hear God clearly.
  - Visions without instruction can produce fear, confusion, and wrong conclusions.
  - God desires relationship, not just outward obedience.
  - Spiritual growth begins with listening to God deeply.

application:
  - Pray for spiritual ears, not only spiritual sight
  - Slow down and listen before reacting
  - Seek God's instruction before making conclusions
  - Test impressions through Scripture and wisdom
  - Stay humble and teachable before God

prayer: Father, open my spiritual ears to hear Your voice clearly. Give me wisdom, discernment, and understanding so I may follow Your instruction faithfully.
