title: Instruction Before Action

summary: Wisdom and discernment help believers seek God's direction before making important decisions.

topics:
  - hearing
  - wisdom

keywords:
  - instruction
  - wisdom
  - discernment
  - guidance
  - obedience
  - patience

scripture:
  ref: "Proverbs 19:20"
  text: "Hear counsel, and receive instruction, that thou mayest be wise in thy latter end."

truth: Godly wisdom grows through receiving instruction and guidance before acting.

insight:
  - Rushed decisions often create unnecessary problems.
  - Instruction helps believers avoid confusion and instability.
  - Wisdom develops through humility and teachability.
  - God provides guidance through Scripture and discernment.

application:
  - Seek God's wisdom before major decisions
  - Remain teachable and humble
  - Practice patience instead of rushing
  - Study Scripture consistently for guidance

prayer: Father, teach me to seek Your instruction before acting so I may walk in wisdom and discernment.
