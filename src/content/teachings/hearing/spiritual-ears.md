title: Spiritual Ears

summary: God desires believers to develop spiritual sensitivity and attentiveness to His voice and truth.

topics:
  - hearing
  - discernment

keywords:
  - spiritual ears
  - hearing God
  - discernment
  - wisdom
  - guidance

scripture:
  ref: "Matthew 11:15"
  text: "He that hath ears to hear, let him hear."

truth: Spiritual sensitivity is necessary for recognizing God's guidance and truth.

insight:
  - Spiritual hearing requires humility and attentiveness.
  - God speaks through Scripture, wisdom, and conviction.
  - Distractions can weaken spiritual attentiveness.
  - Discernment grows through consistent relationship with God.

application:
  - Spend regular time in prayer and Scripture
  - Reduce distractions that hinder spiritual focus
  - Remain sensitive to conviction and wisdom
  - Practice listening before reacting

prayer: Father, develop spiritual sensitivity within me so I may hear Your voice clearly and walk faithfully in Your truth.
