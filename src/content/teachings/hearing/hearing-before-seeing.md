title: Hearing Before Seeing

summary: Spiritual understanding often begins by hearing God's voice before fully seeing His direction.

topics:
  - hearing
  - guidance

keywords:
  - hearing God
  - guidance
  - discernment
  - instruction
  - wisdom
  - obedience

scripture:
  ref: "Romans 10:17"
  text: "So then faith cometh by hearing, and hearing by the word of God."

truth: Faith and spiritual direction begin through hearing and responding to God's Word.

insight:
  - God often speaks before revealing the full picture.
  - Spiritual growth develops through listening and trust.
  - Hearing God's truth strengthens faith and discernment.
  - Obedience begins with attentive listening.

application:
  - Spend time listening through prayer and Scripture
  - Trust God's direction even when unclear
  - Develop spiritual attentiveness daily
  - Respond to God's guidance with obedience

prayer: Father, help me hear Your voice clearly and trust Your guidance even before I fully understand the path ahead.
