scripture:
  ref: "Romans 12:1"
  text: "I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of God, that ye present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God, which is your reasonable service."

truth: Biblical sacrifice involves surrendering every part of life to God in worship and obedience.

insight:
  - Sacrifice in Scripture was connected to devotion and surrender.
  - God desires wholehearted obedience, not empty rituals.
  - Worship often requires personal surrender.
  - Spiritual growth deepens through faithful obedience.

application:
  - Surrender your life continually to God
  - Practice obedience even when difficult
  - Let worship flow from sincerity
  - Pursue spiritual growth with humility

prayer: Father, help me live as a willing sacrifice before You with sincerity, obedience, and wholehearted devotion.
