title: Salvation Was Not Free

summary: Salvation is freely offered to believers, but it came through the costly sacrifice of Christ.

topics:
  - sacrifice
  - salvation

keywords:
  - salvation
  - grace
  - sacrifice
  - redemption
  - Jesus
  - cross

scripture:
  ref: "1 Peter 1:18-19"
  text: "Forasmuch as ye know that ye were not redeemed with corruptible things, as silver and gold... But with the precious blood of Christ."

truth: Salvation came through the costly sacrifice and precious blood of Jesus Christ.

insight:
  - Redemption required the sacrifice of Christ.
  - God's grace reveals both mercy and holiness.
  - The cross demonstrates the depth of God's love.
  - Salvation should never be treated casually.

application:
  - Live with gratitude for salvation
  - Honor Christ through obedience
  - Remember the cost of redemption
  - Walk faithfully with humility

prayer: Father, thank You for the priceless gift of salvation through Jesus Christ. Help me live with gratitude, humility, and faithful obedience.
