---
id: unique-assignment
title: You Are Made for a Specific Assignment

summary: God created every person with a unique purpose and path to walk in.

topics: ["purpose", "identity"]
keywords: ["assignment", "calling", "purpose", "comparison", "uniqueness"]

scripture:
  ref: "Ephesians 2:10"
  text: "For we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus unto good works, which God hath before ordained that we should walk in them."

truth: God has given each person a different purpose, so comparing yourself with others can keep you from seeing your own calling clearly.

insight:
  - God created you with gifts, experiences, and strengths that are unique to your life.
  - Focusing too much on other people can distract you from the purpose God has for you.

application:
  - Focus on the path God has placed before you
  - Appreciate the way God created you
  - Avoid comparing your journey with someone else's

prayer: Lord, help me trust the purpose You have for my life and walk faithfully in the path You have prepared for me.
---
