keywords:

scripture:
  ref: "Luke 12:48"
  text: "For unto whomsoever much is given, of him shall be much required."

truth: God holds spiritual leaders accountable for how they steward influence, wisdom, and responsibility.

insight:
  - Leadership involves service, not personal exaltation.
  - Greater responsibility requires greater wisdom and humility.
  - Spiritual influence affects the lives of others deeply.
  - Faithful stewardship honors God and strengthens others.

application:
  - Lead with humility and integrity
  - Practice accountability consistently
  - Serve others faithfully
  - Seek wisdom before making decisions

prayer: Father, help me carry responsibility with wisdom, humility, and faithfulness so my life honors You and strengthens others.
