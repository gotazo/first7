Daniel records a blessing connected with reaching one thousand three hundred and thirty-five days.

The number appears in the context of future prophetic events.

Daniel's prophecy links 1260, 1290, and 1335 as related prophetic periods.

Many interpretations have been proposed, but the passage encourages patience and faithfulness while awaiting God's fulfillment of prophecy.
