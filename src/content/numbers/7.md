## Basic Meaning

The number seven appears frequently throughout Scripture and is often associated with completion, fullness, and finished work.

---

## Biblical Usage

God completed creation and rested on the seventh day.

Seven priests carrying seven trumpets marched around Jericho before the city fell.

The book of Revelation contains references to seven churches, seven seals, seven trumpets, and seven bowls.

---

## Summary

Throughout Scripture, the number seven is repeatedly connected with completion, fulfillment, and the accomplishment of God's purposes.
