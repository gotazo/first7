## Basic Meaning

The number seven appears frequently throughout Scripture and is often associated with completion, fullness, and finished work.

---

## Biblical Usage

God completed creation and rested on the seventh day.

Seven trumpets were used during the fall of Jericho.

Revelation contains references to seven churches, seven seals, seven trumpets, and seven bowls.
