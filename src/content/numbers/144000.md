## Basic Meaning

The number 144000 appears in Revelation in connection with a sealed group identified from the tribes of Israel.

---

## Biblical Usage

Revelation 7 and Revelation 14 contain references to the 144000.

Interpretations differ regarding the precise nature of this group.
