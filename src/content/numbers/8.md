## Basic Meaning

The number eight is often associated with new beginnings and a new phase following completion.

---

## Biblical Usage

Circumcision was performed on the eighth day.

Eight people were preserved through the Flood in Noah's ark.

The number appears in contexts involving renewal and a new beginning.
