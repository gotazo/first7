## Basic Meaning

The number 490 is derived from seventy weeks and is associated with Daniel's prophecy concerning Israel and Jerusalem.

---

## Biblical Usage

Daniel 9 records the prophecy of seventy weeks.

The prophecy contains one of the most discussed prophetic timelines in Scripture.
