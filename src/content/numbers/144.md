## Basic Meaning

The number 144 appears in Revelation in connection with measurements associated with the New Jerusalem.

---

## Biblical Usage

Revelation records a wall measuring one hundred forty-four cubits.

The number reflects the repeated use of twelve and its multiples within the vision of the New Jerusalem.
