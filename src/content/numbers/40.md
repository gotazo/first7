## Basic Meaning

The number forty frequently appears during periods of testing, preparation, waiting, and transition.

---

## Biblical Usage

The Flood lasted forty days and nights.

Moses spent forty days on Mount Sinai.

Israel wandered forty years in the wilderness.

Jesus fasted forty days in the wilderness.
