## Basic Meaning

The number twenty-four is frequently associated with worship, service, and heavenly order.

---

## Biblical Usage

David organized the priesthood into twenty-four divisions.

Revelation describes twenty-four elders surrounding God's throne.

The number appears in contexts involving worship and service.
