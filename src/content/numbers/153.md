## Basic Meaning

The number one hundred fifty-three appears in a specific historical event recorded in John's Gospel.

## Biblical Usage

After His resurrection, Jesus directed the disciples to cast their net.

The disciples caught one hundred fifty-three fish.

The number is recorded as part of the miracle narrative.
