## Basic Meaning

The number seventy-two appears in some Bible translations of Luke's account of the disciples sent by Jesus.

---

## Biblical Usage

Luke records Jesus sending out disciples to minister and proclaim the kingdom.

Some manuscripts record seventy while others record seventy-two.

Students should be aware of the textual variation.
