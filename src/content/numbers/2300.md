## Basic Meaning

The number 2300 appears in Daniel's vision concerning future events.

---

## Biblical Usage

Daniel records a period of two thousand three hundred days.

The prophecy is connected with the cleansing of the sanctuary.

Various interpretations have been proposed throughout church history.
