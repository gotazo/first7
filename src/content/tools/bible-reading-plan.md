## Build a Consistent Bible Reading Habit

Create a personalized Bible reading plan based on your schedule, pace, and spiritual goals. Whether you want to read through the entire Bible in a year, focus on the New Testament, or create a slower custom plan, this tool helps you stay consistent in Scripture.

Track your daily progress, print your plan, and return anytime to continue where you left off.

> “Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path.”
> — Psalm 119:105

## Features

* Full Bible, Old Testament, or New Testament plans
* Flexible reading durations
* Custom reading schedules
* Progress tracking
* Printable reading plans
* Automatically saved progress

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does my progress save automatically?

Yes. Your reading progress is automatically saved locally in your browser.

### Can I print my Bible reading plan?

Yes. Use the print button inside the tool to create a printable version.

### Can I create a custom reading duration?

Yes. You can choose preset plans or enter your own custom number of days.

### Will my progress stay after refreshing the page?

Yes. Your progress remains saved unless you manually reset the plan or clear browser storage.
